I built a Claude skill that finds the story behind your next LinkedIn post in 5 mins. But does it without writing a single word for you. Here's how it works:

1. You give it a topic. Something you want to write about but can't find an angle for.

2. Then it interviews you. It starts by asking for a specific moment like a client or a conversation or something you saw or felt around that topic.

3. If you're stuck which most people are, it walks you back through time. Last week or last month or 3 months ago or even a year or all the way back to when you first started if need be. Each prompt gives your brain one specific place to search for an idea instead of literally anything that ever existed.

4. If there's still no single moment, it pulls in your credibility / earned perspective like the stuff you've seen across years in your field / industry that only you and a few others could claim.

5. Once it finds something real it starts digging. What happened right before? What did they actually say? How did you feel? What changed for you?

6. Then it hands it all back as a set of notes. The moment, detail, realisation and the lesson.

But what happens NEXT is the best bit:

It stops. It doesn't write the post. You do.

That's the whole point.
